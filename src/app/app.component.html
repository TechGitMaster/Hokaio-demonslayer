How did Muzan become a Demon

Muzan became a demon because of the incomplete treatment he received from a generous doctor. His doctor was trying to save him from dying due to an unknown mortal disease. However, Muzan ends up killing the doctor.

First let’s see who Muzan really was. He was a young frail boy born with a sickness. He was informed that he wouldn’t live to be even 20. The medicines then did nothing to cure him. Later on a doctor visited him claiming to have a medicine which can cure him. Desperate to live, Muzan took that medicine but his health didn’t improve soon.
